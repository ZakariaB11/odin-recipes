Pizza recipe

For a large (14-inch) homemade pepperoni pizza, you will need 16–24 oz of dough, 1/2–1 cup of pizza sauce, 3–4 cups of shredded mozzarella, and 20–30 slices of pepperoni. Bake at 500F (260 C) on a preheated stone or pan for 10-14 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

  • 16–24 oz of dough
  • 1/2–1 cup of pizza sauce
  • 3–4 cups of shredded mozzarella
  • 20–30 slices of pepperoni
  1. Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven and preheat to its highest setting
  2. On a floured surface or parchment paper, stretch or roll the dough into a 14-inch circle.
  3. Spread sauce evenly, leaving 1 inch for the crust
  4. Sprinkle a small amount of cheese first, then add the rest (this helps keep toppings in place).
  5. Distribute pepperoni slices over the cheese.
  6. Transfer the pizza (with parchment paper) to the preheated stone. Bake for 10-14 minutes until the crust is browned and cheese is melted and slightly browned. Let it cool for 5 minutes.
