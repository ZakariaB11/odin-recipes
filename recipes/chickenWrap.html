Chicken wrap recipe

Chicken wraps are quick, versatile meals featuring cooked chicken (grilled, fried, or shredded) combined with vegetables, cheese, and sauces—such as ranch, buffalo, or garlic sauce—tightly rolled in a flour tortilla.

  • Cooked Chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Shredded Cheese
  • BBQ sauce or Algerian sauce
  • Flour Tortillas
  1. Fold the outsides of the wrap in on itself, Then roll it up.
  2. Place it seam side down on the hot grill.
  3. Flip it over after 2 to 3 minutes, and grill it for 2 to 3 more minutes.
